# Sharing to Bluesky

Chive is built on AT Protocol, the same network that powers Bluesky. You can share preprints, reviews, and discussions directly to Bluesky.

## Connecting your Bluesky account

If you signed into Chive with your Bluesky account, you're already connected. Otherwise:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Connected Accounts**
2. Click **Connect Bluesky**
3. Authorize Chive to post on your behalf

## Sharing a preprint

### Quick share

1. On any preprint page, click **Share**
2. Select **Share to Bluesky**
3. A compose window opens with:
   - Paper title
   - Authors
   - Link to the Chive page
   - Optional abstract excerpt
4. Edit the post text if desired
5. Click **Post**

### Share with comment

Add your thoughts when sharing:

1. Click **Share** → **Share to Bluesky**
2. Write your comment above the paper card
3. The paper appears as an embedded card in your post
4. Click **Post**

### Sharing from your profile

Share your own papers:

1. Go to your profile
2. Find the paper
3. Click the share icon
4. Select **Share to Bluesky**

## Sharing reviews

When you write a review on Chive, you can share it to Bluesky:

1. After submitting your review, click **Share Review**
2. The post includes:
   - Your review summary
   - Link to the full review thread
   - Paper title and authors
3. Edit if needed, then **Post**

### Quote-posting reviews

To share someone else's review:

1. On the review, click **Share**
2. Select **Quote to Bluesky**
3. Add your commentary
4. **Post**

## Backlinks

When someone mentions a Chive preprint on Bluesky, it creates a backlink:

### Viewing backlinks

1. Go to any preprint page
2. Click the **Bluesky** tab
3. See all Bluesky posts mentioning this paper

### Backlink types

| Type    | Description                      |
| ------- | -------------------------------- |
| Mention | Post contains the Chive URL      |
| Quote   | Post embeds the paper card       |
| Reply   | Response to a Chive-related post |

### Backlink metrics

The preprint page shows:

- Total Bluesky mentions
- Recent posts
- Notable accounts discussing the paper

## Mentions and autocomplete

### Mentioning authors

When composing a Bluesky post through Chive:

1. Type `@` to mention someone
2. Autocomplete shows:
   - Paper authors (if they have Bluesky handles)
   - Your Bluesky follows
3. Select to insert their handle

### Mentioning preprints

Paste a Chive URL into your post:

1. The URL automatically converts to a rich card
2. The card shows title, authors, and abstract preview
3. Clicking the card opens the paper on Chive

## Notifications

### Bluesky activity

When someone interacts with your shared content on Bluesky:

- Likes, reposts, and replies appear in your Chive notifications
- Click to view the activity on Bluesky

### Chive activity

When someone engages with your paper on Chive:

- Reviews, endorsements, and comments appear in your Chive notifications
- Optionally share these to Bluesky

## Privacy and control

### What Chive can do

With your permission, Chive can:

- Post to Bluesky on your behalf
- Read your Bluesky profile (handle, display name)
- Access public posts for backlink detection

### What Chive cannot do

- Read your DMs
- Post without your explicit action
- Access your followers list
- Modify your Bluesky profile

### Revoking access

1. Go to **Settings** → **Connected Accounts**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to Bluesky
3. Or revoke from Bluesky: **Settings** → **App Passwords**

## Share settings

| Setting                     | Options                    | Default         |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------- | --------------- |
| Default share destination   | Bluesky / Copy link / Both | Both            |
| Include abstract in shares  | Yes / No / First 100 chars | First 100 chars |
| Auto-share my reviews       | Never / Ask / Always       | Ask             |
| Show backlinks on my papers | Yes / No                   | Yes             |

## Troubleshooting

### Post failed to send

- Check your Bluesky connection in Settings
- Verify your Bluesky account is in good standing
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting

### Backlinks not appearing

- Backlinks are indexed every few minutes
- The post must contain the full Chive URL
- Private Bluesky posts are not indexed

### Card preview not loading

- Wait a few seconds for the preview to generate
- Check that the preprint URL is correct
- Some older preprints may not have preview images
